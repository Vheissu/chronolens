About Chronolens

Upload a photo and transform it into different eras using AI. Built for hackathon fun — fast, simple, and privacy‑friendly.

Usage & Limits

  • Guest mode: up to 10 generations per day (automatic anonymous sign‑in).
  • Signed‑in: up to 25 generations per day.
  • Generations reset daily (San Francisco time).

What You Can Do

  • Upload a photo and choose an era (e.g., 1920, 1970, 2090).
  • Keep people in the scene while transforming the surroundings.
  • Compare before/after with an interactive slider.
  • Save your results and optionally share a view‑only link.
